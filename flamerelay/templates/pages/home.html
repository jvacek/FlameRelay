Get a lighter with a QR code? Congrats! You're now part of FlameRelay.
It's a fun project to track a lighter's journey across the world. Here's how it works.

🔍 Find a lighter and see where it's been
📍 Submit a photo with a location and a little message
🤲 Pass it on to a stranger or a friend
📩 Get an update when it's found again (if you want).
