How to make the most of this?

Who should I give the lighter to?

Anyone you want. It's your lighter, you decide. I recommend giving it to someone who you think will enjoy silliness, and can further push the lighter far. Maybe someone who's been having a rough time lately?
You can also try leave it in a busy place like at a bar, a club smoking room, airport lounges, or any other place you think like-minded people might end up int

Anything I should do with the lighter?

If you could, please check that the lighter has enough fluid/gas, If you can, please top it up. This way it can travel just that bit further. You can also doodle on it, or decorate it in any way you want.

What do I do if the lighter died?

If you can, please refill it. If you can't, please pass it on to someone else.
