What happens next

You're now following this lighter — you'll get an email every time someone else checks it in. One thing to know: each person can only check in once per lighter, so once you hand it off and the next person checks in, you won't be able to add more check-ins. You're passing the pen as well as the flame.

Tips for a good handoff

— Ask the next person to check in right when they receive it, while you're still together. It's much easier than chasing them later.
— Give it to someone curious or travelling — someone who'll actually pass it on rather than pocket it.
— Top up the lighter before you hand it over. A full lighter is easier to pass on than a dead one.
— No one to hand it to directly? Leave it somewhere public — a bar, a hostel, a festival. Whoever picks it up becomes the next keeper.
